# Naming and Metadata

## Primary Recommendation

### Name
**OrchLayer GPT**

### Description

Structured orchestration for human-AI and AI-AI collaboration. Helps with planning, context architecture, review, drift control, session continuity, and handoff packaging. Best used inside Projects for long-running work.

### Metadata Line
**L1 orchestration for structured collaboration**

---

## Alternative Names

### 1. L1 Orchestrator GPT
Good when you want maximum explicitness and architectural precision.

### 2. Context Architect GPT
Good when you want a slightly more premium or strategy-oriented identity.

### 3. Orchestration Layer GPT
Good when you want clearer descriptive naming, though it is longer.

---

## Naming Guidance

Prefer names that are:
- clear
- easy to remember
- professional
- aligned with orchestration and continuity

Avoid names that are:
- overly flashy
- vague
- too generic
- strongly tied to one narrow workflow unless that is intentional

---

## Recommended Final Choice

Use **OrchLayer GPT** unless you specifically want the more technical label **L1 Orchestrator GPT**.
